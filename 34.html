Travel Checklist

  1. Make travel reservations
  2. Pack essential documents and items:
    1. Passport
    2. Travel tickets
    3. Travel itinerary
    4. Travel insurance
  3. Pack personal items:
    1. Clothes
    2. Toiletries
    3. Medications
  4. Prepare travel finances:
    1. Money and credit cards
  5. Bring necessary electronic devices:
    1. Mobile phone and charger
    2. Travel adapter
  6. Plan for navigation and exploration:
    1. Maps or GPS
    2. Travel guides
